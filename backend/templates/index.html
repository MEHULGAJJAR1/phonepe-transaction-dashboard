ABOUT THE PROJECT

Understanding India's Digital Payment Revolution

PhonePe Pulse dataset provides deep insights into digital payment trends across India. It covers transactions, users, and insurance data at country, state, district, and pincode levels from 2018 to 2024.

This dashboard helps analyze transaction growth, top performing states, and payment category distribution — giving a clear picture of India's evolving fintech landscape.
